## What This Skill Does

Generates a complete SaaS user onboarding and activation email system — from first login through feature adoption, re-engagement, and churn rescue. Built for SaaS founders, growth marketers, and customer success teams who need high-converting onboarding sequences without hiring a specialist.

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1: User Journey Map & Activation Blueprint
Maps your ICP (ideal customer profile) through their first 30 days. Identifies the moment of value (the "aha moment"), key activation milestones, and the drop-off points where users churn before converting. Output includes a milestone map, behavioral trigger list, and email timing recommendations.

### Prompt 2: Welcome & Activation Sequence (Days 0–14)
A 7-email sequence timed to the user's first 14 days. Each email has: subject line (3 variants A/B/C), preview text, body copy, CTA, and a behavioral trigger (what action sends the next email vs. what sends a re-engagement). Includes a branch for activated users (skip to nurture) vs. stuck users (push toward activation).

### Prompt 3: Feature Adoption Campaign
3-email drip sequence for each key feature users aren't adopting. Uses the "show-don't-tell" framework: Email 1 = the problem the feature solves, Email 2 = social proof (case study or stat), Email 3 = direct invitation + offer. Also generates 2 in-app message variants per feature (tooltip + modal) for teams using Intercom/Appcues/Pendo.

### Prompt 4: Re-engagement & Churn Rescue Kit
Three separate 4-email sequences for: (1) At-risk users (7-14 days inactive, still subscribed), (2) Churned users (within 30 days of cancellation), (3) Win-back campaign (churned 31-90 days ago). Each sequence includes a "break-up email" as the final send — the counterintuitive closer that recovers 10-15% of otherwise lost accounts.

## Inputs Required

- **Product name and one-line description**
- **ICP:** job title, company size, primary pain point
- **Pricing model:** free trial / freemium / paid only
- **3 core features** (with brief description of each)
- **Moment of value:** what action signals a user "gets it" (e.g., "first report run", "first team member invited")
- **Brand voice:** formal/casual/playful/direct

## Outputs Delivered

- User journey map with milestone timeline and churn risk flags
- 7-email welcome + activation sequence (21 subject line variants)
- Feature adoption drip (3 emails + 2 in-app messages per feature = up to 15 assets for 3 features)
- 3 re-engagement sequences × 4 emails = 12 additional emails
- Total: 30+ email assets per run
