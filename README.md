# SaaS Onboarding & Activation Email Sequence Generator

Generate a complete user onboarding and activation email system for any SaaS product in minutes. 30+ email assets, branching logic, feature adoption campaigns, and a full churn rescue kit.

Most SaaS products lose 40-60% of trial users before they ever activate. The typical fix — hiring a CRM specialist or email copywriter — costs $3,000-$10,000 and takes weeks. Generic templates from Mailchimp or ConvertKit don't understand your product, your ICP, or your moment of value.

## What You Get

Run 4 prompts. Get:

**Prompt 1 — User Journey Map & Activation Blueprint**
- ICP profile with behavioral assumptions
- Day-by-day milestone timeline (Day 0 → Day 30)
- "Aha moment" definition + leading indicators
- Drop-off risk points with prevention triggers
- Email cadence recommendation (timing + trigger logic)

**Prompt 2 — Welcome & Activation Sequence (Days 0-14)**
- 7 emails with full body copy
- 3 subject line variants per email (A/B/C test-ready)
- Preview text for each email
- Behavioral branching: activated path vs. stuck path
- CTA hierarchy (primary + secondary)

**Prompt 3 — Feature Adoption Campaign**
- 3 emails per feature (problem → proof → invitation)
- 2 in-app message variants per feature (tooltip + modal)
- Send trigger logic (what user behavior triggers each email)
- Works with Intercom, Appcues, Pendo, Customer.io

**Prompt 4 — Re-engagement & Churn Rescue**
- At-risk sequence (7-14 days inactive): 4 emails
- Churned sequence (within 30 days): 4 emails
- Win-back campaign (31-90 days out): 4 emails
- Break-up email for each sequence (the one that actually re-engages)

## How to Use

Copy each prompt into Claude (or any capable LLM). Fill in the bracketed variables. Paste the output into your email platform (Customer.io, Intercom, Drip, Klaviyo, HubSpot, etc.).
